     ### First aid kit

     Distribute these to crew or keep them around the submarine if you have the resources.

     - {{BANDAGES}}
     - {{SUTURES}}
     - {{TOURNIQUET}}s
     - {{AUTOPULSE}} - only used to keep yourself conscious till reaching a doctor
     - {{BLOOD_PACKS}} - Preferably O- or O+
     - {{GYPSUM}}
     - {{TRAUMA_SHEARS}}
     - {{OPIUM}} to use with a {{WRENCH}} on {{DISLOCATIONS}} (if your skill is low)

     It can't be placed in a med kit, but a {{WRENCH}} is used for {{DISLOCATIONS}}, which are common
     <br><br>
     #### Doctor's med kit 1

     - {{OPIUM}} or {{MORPHINE}}
     - {{BANDAGES}}
     - {{SUTURES}} - fits in surgery toolbox instead if you carry one
     - {{TOURNIQUET}}
     - {{HEALTH_SCANNER}}
     - {{HEMATOLOGY_ANALYZER}}
     - {{BLOOD_PACKS}} - Preferably O- or O+
     - {{GYPSUM}}
     - {{TRAUMA_SHEARS}} - fits in surgery toolbox instead if you carry one

     This first med kit will cover the most basic diagnosis and treatments, but a second med kit and/or surgery kit provides most of the tools for more serious treatments.

     It's also recommended to keep a stasis bag in your hotbar
     
     <br><br>
     #### Doctor's med kit 2

     - {{SURGICAL_DRILL}}
     - {{SURGICAL_SAW}}
     - {{AMBUBAG}}
     - {{ANTISEPTIC_SPRAYER}} with {{ANTISEPTIC}} loaded or {{ANTIBIOTIC_OINTMENT}}
     - {{MANUAL_DEFIBRILLATOR}} or {{AED}}
     - {{MANNITOL}}
     - {{BROAD_SPECTRUM_ANTIBIOTICS}}
     - Calyxanide
     - {{PLASTISEAL}}  or {{ANTIBIOTIC_GLUE}}
     
     <br><br>
     #### Surgery kit

     - {{SCALPEL}}
     - {{HEMOSTAT}}
     - {{SKIN_RETRACTORS}}
     - {{SUTURES}}
     - {{TWEEZERS}}
     - {{NEEDLE}}
     - {{DRAINAGE}}
     - {{MULTIPURPOSE_SCALPEL}} or an extra {{NEEDLE}} and {{DRAINAGE}}

     Consider adding a {{NEEDLE}} to your med kit if you don't carry around a surgery kit regularly
