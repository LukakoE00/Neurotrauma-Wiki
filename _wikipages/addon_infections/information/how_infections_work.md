     #### Old:
     In base Neurotrauma, infections develop from wounds, which are limb specific. After a certain point, {{INFECTED_WOUNDS}} lead to {{SEPSIS}}, which {{BROAD_SPECTRUM_ANTIBIOTICS}} trivially cures. NT Infections changes this by adding several new infections and changing how infections progress.

     <br>
     #### New:
     NT Infections starts the same way. When a wound is not disinfected or bandaged, it can become infected. Infected wounds have a chance of developing into a proper limb infection. The infection will build up and have a chance to enter the bloodstream, causing a blood infection. Severe enough infections may then cause {{SEPSIS}}.

     There are several signs/symptoms that a limb is infected:

     - {{INFLAMMATION}}
     - {{ABSCESS}}
     - {{PURULENT_DRAINAGE}}

     <br>
     Limb infections may vary from moderate to severe and may also cause other diseases.
