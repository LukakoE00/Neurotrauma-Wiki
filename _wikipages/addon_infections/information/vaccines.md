     A useful tool to preemptively fight off an infection. Vaccines give your body a massive advantage during the defense stage.

     <br>
     #### Bacterial Vaccines:
     For bacterial vaccines, you can craft it by using 3 confirmed samples, {{ANAPARALYZANT}}, and {{SALINE}} to make 2 vaccines.

     <br>
     #### Viral Vaccines:
     For viral vaccines, a sample, {{TONIC_LIQUID}}, and {{SALINE}} will craft 2.

     <br>
     #### Nota bene:
     - Vaccines do not render patients completely immune to an infection, but instead provide a bonus for fighting against it. Vaccines do not work if immunity is compromised
     - Vaccines will lose efficacy over time, diminishing its effects on fighting against an infection. Consider keeping vaccinations up to date
     - Vaccines do not work immediately and must require some time before becoming fully effective
     - Most of the vaccines in this mod are completely fictional
